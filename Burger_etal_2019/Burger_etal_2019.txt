Quality Notes: 

1. Reference(s) column is provided for each brain mass.  See full reference information in gyz043_suppl_supplement_material.docx (this could be linked in a matrix / database for metadata).

2. It was not possible to reproduce the derived variables, Brain.resd and T_Resid, even after consulting the publication and the author.  The author suggested to use a newer tree and derive them anew.

3. Methods Notes:
For published datasets, we verified references for accuracy and merged data into a master file standardized by the taxonomy in Wilson and Reeder (2005)...
When subspecies were reported, we took mean values for species weighted for sample sizes.
(!) We used a conversion of 1 g to 1 cm3 when different units were reported following earlier studies (e.g., Isler and van Schaik 2009).
