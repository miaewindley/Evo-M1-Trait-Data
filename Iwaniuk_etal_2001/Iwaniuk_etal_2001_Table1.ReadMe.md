# Iwaniuk et al. 2001 — Table 1 (order-level EQ and play)

Iwaniuk AN, Nelson JE, Pellis SM (2001). *Do big-brained animals play more? Comparative analyses
of play and relative brain size in mammals.* J Comp Psychol 115(1):29–41.
doi:10.1037/0735-7036.115.1.29

Full table title: **"Table 1. Encephalization Quotients (EQs) and Play Scores for 15 Mammalian
Orders."** Order-level summary (15 rows). Not related to the Heffner digital-dexterity data —
this paper is about play behaviour and relative brain size.
